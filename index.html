About Us

Buckeye Hackers is a student organization at the Ohio State University, founded in Spring of 2015. We are actively working to promote hacker culture. We define hackers as anyone with a passion for programming or otherwise solving problems in new and creative ways. Our biggest project is the OHI/O fall hackathon that we will be planning every year. We meet every week on Mondays at 7pm in Caldwell Labs 120, where we plan the hackathon every other week and have code nights on the other weeks.
